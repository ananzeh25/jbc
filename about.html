Our Story

JBC Maintenance & Repair was founded with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable truck repair services to the hardworking drivers and fleet operators of Salt Lake City and beyond.

What started as a small operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in truck repair across Utah. Our success is built on a foundation of integrity, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to getting our customers back on the road as quickly as possible.

Located at 1425 Industrial Rd in Salt Lake City, our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and staffed by certified technicians who specialize in diesel engines and heavy-duty truck systems. But we're not just about fixing trucksâ€”we're about building relationships.
